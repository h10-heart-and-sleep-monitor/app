Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Data Protection at a Glance

Protecting your health data is an important concern to us. This app has been designed so that it does not store any personal data on external servers.

2. Collection of Health Data (ECG & Pulse)

The app receives data from your Polar H10 chest strap via Bluetooth.

  • Local Storage: All measurements are stored exclusively on your smartphone locally.
  • No Cloud Sync: No automatic transmission to servers or third-party providers takes place.
  • PDF Export: If you use the PDF export function, the file is created locally on your device. You have full control over whether and with whom you share this file.

3. Permissions

The app requires access to Bluetooth (Scan & Connect) to establish a connection to the sensor. Without this permission, the core function of the app cannot be used.

4. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can remove it at any time by deleting the app or within the app's diary function.
