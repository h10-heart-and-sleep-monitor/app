Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Data Protection at a Glance

This app is designed to store no personal data on external servers. Your health data stays on your device.

2. Collection of Health Data

The app receives data from your Polar H10 via Bluetooth.

  • Local Storage: All measurements are stored exclusively on your smartphone.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not transmit data to any servers or third parties.
  • PDF Export: Files are generated locally. You decide if and with whom you share them.

3. Permissions

Bluetooth access is required to connect to the sensor. Without this, the app cannot function.
