Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Data Protection at a Glance

Protecting your health data is vital. This app **does not store data on external servers**.

2. Collection of Health Data

  • Local Storage: All measurements stay exclusively on your smartphone.
  • No Cloud Sync: No transmission to us or third parties.
  • PDF Export: Files are created locally; you decide if you want to share them.

3. Permissions (Bluetooth & Location)

The app requires access to Bluetooth and Location:

  • Bluetooth: To connect to the Polar H10 sensor.
  • Location: Android mandates this to scan for Bluetooth devices. The app **never** tracks or stores your actual physical location.

4. Your Rights

You can remove all data at any time by deleting the app or within the history function.

5. International Privacy & Children

CCPA / California: We do not sell any data.
COPPA / Children: This app is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
